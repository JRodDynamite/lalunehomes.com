La Lune Homes is located in the Heart of Fort Kochi in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments.

 

  • Rooms & Rates

    La Lune features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi throughout the property. Free private parking is available on site. Enjoy a cup of tea from your terrace or balcony. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hairdryer.

     

  • Heart of Fort Kochi

    La Lune is located near to the prime sites within Fort Cochin. Within just 200 meters from the Chinese fishing nets, St.Francis Church and the Beach, La Lune is your base to explore the heritage and culture of Fort Kochi.

     

  • Continental Breakfast

    On request, enjoy Buffet breakfast meals in an open terrance. Meals will consist of a rich array of spices with a combination of seasonal local produce to create soul satisfying dishes.
