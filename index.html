La Lune Homes is located in the Heart of Fort Kochi in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments.

 

  • Rooms Features

    La Lune provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and optional private parking. Rooms are spacious, comfortable, and feature natural light, flat-screen TVs, and private balconies. Bathrooms offer 24-hour hot water, free toiletries, and hairdryers.

     

  • Heart of Fort Kochi

    La Lune Fort Kochi is ideally situated near the key attractions of Fort Kochi. Within a mere 200 meters from the Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, and the beach, La Lune offers the perfect base to immerse yourself in the rich heritage and culture of Kochi and Kerala as a whole.

     

  • Continental Breakfast

    Upon request, enjoy a delightful buffet breakfast on our open terrace. Our meals feature a rich array of spices combined with seasonal local produce, creating soul-satisfying dishes. We serve organic eggs sourced from our free-range poultry farm and offer a selection of in-house grown local fruits.

Welcome to La Lune Fort Kochi

La Lune offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi throughout the property. Free private parking is available on request. Each room is specifically designed to deliver utmost comfort with a super friendly ambiance. All rooms are spacious and airy, allowing access to natural light, and are equipped with flat TV screens and private bathrooms that provide 24-hour hot and cold water. For your convenience, you'll find free toiletries and a hairdryer in every bathroom. Each bedroom also features an individual balcony.

Click HERE! to watch a video of the premises.

Guests have access to a common sitting area and a pantry stocked with basic essentials like tea, coffee, milk etc. The pantry is equipped with a fridge, kettle, microwave, and RO and UV water filters. This common central space also fosters interaction among guests from different parts of the world.

The property also boasts a garden terrace with tables, chairs, and sunbeds. This space is commonly used for our guests' breakfast, group yoga sessions, sunbathing, and more.

Environmentally sustainable practices

Sustainability Initiatives

Our entire property is self-sustained for all its power requirements, sourced through solar panels. We also provide charging points for electric vehicles. Special care is taken for waste segregation and recycling to minimize our environmental impact.

Water Conservation

Drinking water is sourced using rainwater harvesting, which is then filtered through an advanced filtration system, including RO and UV filters, ensuring clean and safe drinking water for all guests.

Entertainment

We offer advance booking for a variety of traditional and cultural shows, including Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, Bharatanatyam, Indian classical music, and Kalari (martial arts). Additionally, we arrange yoga classes and Fort Kochi walking tours to enrich your cultural experience.

Health and Wellness

We arrange bookings with reputed and distinguished traditional Ayurvedic wellness centers. These centers offer a range of authentic Ayurvedic massages with herbal oils, including Kalari (deep tissue massage), Abhyangam (Ayurvedic massage), Marma (pressure points massage) and more.

Package Trips

Based on our guests' requests and suggestions, we offer tailor-made package trips suited to your itinerary. Packages include transportation with experienced, polite, and English-speaking drivers to major tourist spots such as Munnar and Thekkady. We also offer exclusive overnight stays on houseboats (1 or 2 bedrooms) in Alleppey (Backwater photos). Additionally, experience stays in treehouses and mudhouses located in tranquil settings.

  • Trekking in Munnar with licensed and experienced guides. Check out the photos in our gallery.
  • Jeep safari (day/night) for spotting wild elephants and other wildlife. Check out the photos in our gallery.
